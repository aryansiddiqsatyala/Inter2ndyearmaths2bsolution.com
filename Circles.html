Inter 2nd Year Maths - Circles Solutions

Q1: Find the equation of the circle whose center is (2, -3) and radius is 5.
Answer: Using the formula (x - h)² + (y - k)² = r², we get, (x - 2)² + (y + 3)² = 25, ( x² + 4 - 4x ) + ( y² + 9 + 6y ) = 25, x² + y² - 4x + 6y + 13 = 25, x² + y² - 4x + 6y + 13 - 25 = 0, Thus the final Answer is, x² + y² - 4x + 6y - 12 = 0.
Q2: Find the equation of a circle passing through (1, 2), (3, -4), and (5, 6).
Answer: Substitute points in the general equation of a circle x² + y² + 2gx + 2fy + c = 0 and solve for g, f, c.
